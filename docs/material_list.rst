Material List
=============


This is the complete list of materials in Extreme PBR, here you will find all the categories, within them you will find the preview of the material, its name, and also the file.exapack in which it is contained.

.. tip:: To search for the material you are interested in, you can search in the sidebar at the top left, or go to the category where you want to search for the material press `Ctrl + F` and then type the name of the material you are looking for.
